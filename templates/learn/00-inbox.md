# 00-Inbox — What This Is

The inbox is the only rule in this system.

Everything comes here first. Ideas, tasks, links, half-formed thoughts, things people tell you, things you don't know where to put. All of it lands here. You never have to decide where something goes in the moment — you capture it, and deal with it later.

**The inbox is not a to-do list.** It's a holding area. Things stay here for hours or days, not weeks. Your agent helps you clear it regularly.

## What goes here

- Anything you want to remember but don't have time to file
- Links you want to read or save
- Ideas that came to you mid-conversation
- Tasks someone mentioned
- Anything where you're not sure where it belongs

## What doesn't go here

Once you know where something goes, put it there directly. The inbox is for uncertainty and speed, not for things you've already decided on.

## The two files

`quick-notes.md` — one line per thought. Fast. No formatting needed.
`links.md` — URLs with a line of context so you remember why you saved them.

## How it gets cleared

During your weekly review, or when you ask your agent to process it. Your agent will suggest destinations and confirm with you before moving anything.

## The rule

If you only follow one thing in this system, follow this: **everything starts in the inbox**. The rest of the vault stays clean because of this single habit.
